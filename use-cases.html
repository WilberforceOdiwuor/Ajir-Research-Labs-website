Use Cases

Draft review page. These are the most plausible early audiences and workflows for askNoema.

Priority users

  • Researchers and policy analysts who need to inspect claims moving across fast media channels without losing provenance and context.
  • Journalists and investigative teams who need faster first-pass breakdowns of what a clip is claiming, omitting, or reframing.
  • Trust and safety or integrity teams who need structured views of manipulative content, narratives, escalation patterns, and ambiguity.
  • Knowledge workers in high-noise domains who need a way to reason with media streams instead of passively consuming them.

Early workflow candidates

  • Clip inspection for identifying claims, evidence cues, contradictions, and open questions in a single video.
  • Narrative comparison for comparing multiple clips or sources discussing the same event or topic.
  • Session memory for carrying prior analysis forward so insight compounds over time.
  • Reasoning summaries for turning raw media into a concise but inspectable explanation surface.
