Is there a live product?

Not yet. The website currently presents the research direction, product thesis, and company structure while the product remains in design and prototyping.

What is askNoema?

askNoema is the product direction under development at Ajir Research Labs: an AI-native social intelligence platform for turning short-form media into structured, inspectable reasoning support.

Who is this for?

The strongest early fit appears to be researchers, analysts, journalists, integrity teams, and other users who need to work with fast media environments without surrendering judgment.

Is Ajir Research Labs a research organization or a startup?

Both, in practice. The work is research-led, but the objective is to build durable product infrastructure around how people reason with media.
