AI Policy

Status: This policy is currently in draft.

Ajir Research Labs is documenting the operational rules, disclosure standards, and human oversight requirements that govern AI-assisted analysis across its products and research outputs.

Current Operating Position

Until the full public policy is published, Ajir treats AI output as analytical support rather than authoritative judgment. Human review remains responsible for product decisions, legal commitments, and public claims.

Scope

The published AI policy will cover model usage boundaries, attribution and disclosure practices, verification standards, data-handling constraints, and escalation paths for harmful or unreliable output.

Contact

For questions about interim AI governance practices, contact contact@ajirresearch.com.
