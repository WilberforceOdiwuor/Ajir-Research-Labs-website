Careers

Draft review page. Ajir Research Labs is still early, but this is the clearer hiring and talent page the site was missing.

What kind of team this needs

This is not a high-volume hiring page. It is a focused view of the small-team roles that matter most to a research-led AI startup building product, infrastructure, and intellectual clarity at the same time.

Priority profiles

  • Applied ML and multimodal engineers comfortable with evaluation, retrieval, ranking, transcription, and reasoning-oriented system behavior.
  • Frontend and product engineers who can make complex epistemic workflows legible in a fast interface.
  • Infrastructure engineers who can design resilient media pipelines, storage layers, and observability around an evolving system.
  • Research-minded product designers who can turn abstract reasoning requirements into coherent interfaces and product decisions.
  • Startup operators and technical generalists who can close execution gaps across product, research operations, documentation, and team building.

Current status

These roles are draft-facing and under review, not a formal public hiring pipeline yet. For now, the right route is still the contact page.
