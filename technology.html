Technology

Draft review page. This is the technical frame for how Ajir Research Labs thinks about the system, not a production architecture disclosure.

Core technical thesis

The central technical problem is not just media understanding. It is reasoning support under conditions of speed, ambiguity, persuasion, and incomplete evidence. That means the system has to process multimodal inputs without collapsing into generic chat output.

System layers under development

  • Multimodal ingestion for transcript, visual, audio, and metadata capture tied to a live content context.
  • Claim and evidence extraction for distinguishing assertions, cues, uncertainty, and unresolved gaps.
  • Context-locked reasoning for keeping model output bound to the active content state rather than open-ended assistant behavior.
  • Persistent knowledge structures for carrying prior media analysis across sessions and comparisons.

What this page is for

This page gives the website a proper technology slot for future audit, partner review, and eventual public-facing technical explanation. It is intentionally high-level while the underlying implementation is still under development.
