Team

Ajir Research Labs is currently founder-led and built around a research-first view of AI, media, and human reasoning.

Co-Founder

Wilberforce Odiwuor

Co-Founder & CEO, Ajir Research Labs

Wilberforce leads the research and product direction behind Ajir Research Labs and AskNoema. His work centers on structured intelligence, epistemic integrity, and AI systems that help people inspect claims instead of passively consuming conclusions.

The lab’s direction reflects a clear thesis: short-form media moves faster than human verification, so the interface itself should surface context, uncertainty, evidence, and better questions at the moment information is consumed.

Ajir Research Labs is being developed as a long-horizon systems and research effort, with current work focused on multimodal analysis, content-locked reasoning, evidence mapping, and feed-level interfaces that keep human judgment in the loop.

What we’re building toward

  • Multimodal analysis across video, audio, transcript, and metadata so claims are grounded in the actual content being viewed.
  • Reasoning with uncertainty so the system exposes what is known, contested, or missing instead of pretending to have total certainty.
  • Structured outputs over generic chat so users can follow evidence, inspect context, and continue their own inquiry.
